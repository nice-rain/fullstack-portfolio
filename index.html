About

I have always been excited to learn and create new things. My programming background started off at a young age learning how to modify online games. I created several mods for games like Starsige: Tribes and worked on several small projects with friends using Torque Game Engine Advanced. I eventually moved to Dallas and worked as a programmer for an indie game development company for 2 years.

My interest in full stack development started when I began noticing that mobile applications, websites, and games were all able to display the same content delivered by a server. I wanted to learn how to develop front-end and back-end systems to work with a variety of platforms. Lately, I have been working to develop solutions for large corporate clients as well as continuing to advance my skillset to include mobile development as well as machine learning and solutions architecture. To this end, I have also started a developer blog to share my experiences.

Now that you know a little more about me, contact me, and let's create something amazing!
